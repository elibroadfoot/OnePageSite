Facts

Cat's ears can rotate 180 degrees.
An average cat can hear five times better than an average adult human.
There are 88 million pet cats and only 74 million pet dogs in the U.S.
A group of cats is called a clowder.
Adult cats only meow to communicate with humans.
Cats only sweat through their foot pads.
Cats have over 20 muscles that control their ears.
Cats sleep 70% of their lives.
